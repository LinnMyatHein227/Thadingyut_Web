In Thadingyut, the moon takes flight

A silver lantern in the shadow night,

It casts a glow both gentle and bright,

Upon the land, its guiding light.

The pagodas adorned with golden grace,

In festive robes, they find their sacred place,

Buddha blessings shower a warm embrace,

As people gather souls in peaceful chase.

Families reunite with hearts aglow,

In every home the oil lamps softly glow,

The fragrance they show,

Respect and gratitude, in this season, the spark.

The melodies of laughter fill the air,

As offerings and gifts, with love and care,

Bring joy and blessings, none can compare,

Thadingyut, a time of love we share.

In this month, our hearts unite,

To celebrate the victory of radiant light,

Thadingyut, a time that feels so right,

In its warm embrace, we find our endless flight.
